11. Using the Calendar

Using the Calendar

  1. Log In
  2. Navigate to Book Appointment Page
  • Click on the 'Next Month' button to see appointments for next month
  • Click on the 'Prev Month' button to see appointments for previous month
  • Click on the 'Current Month' button to see appointments for current month
  • You cannot book appointment on days with red buttons
  • You can only book days with green buttons
  • There 18 slots for each appointment, once there are 0 slots left, the appointment will become red, and you will not be able to book for that day.
