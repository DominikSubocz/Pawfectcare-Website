Editing Players, Juniors, Members, or Coaches

To edit existing player, junior, member, or coach, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to Respective List:

    2. Go to the list of players, members, coaches, or juniors in the system.

  2. Select Member to Edit:

    3. Either:

    • Tick the checkbox next to the member you want to edit.
    • Navigate to the profile of the member and click the "Update" button.
  3. Click Update Button
  4. Fill in Form:

    5. A form will be presented where you can edit the member's details.
    Leave blank fields if you don't want to update certain information.

  5. Click Submit Button
  6. Return to List:

    7. After submitting, you will be redirected back to the respective list.

Note:
Addresses, doctors, and guardians will be automatically updated when entered in any of these profiles.

Editing Player's Skills

To edit existing skills of player, junior, member, or coach, follow these steps:

  1. Select Player to Edit:

    2. Either:

    • Tick the checkbox next to the player you want to edit skills for.
    • Navigate to the profile of the player and click the "Update Skill" button.
  2. Fill in Form:

    3. A form will be presented where you can edit the player's skills.
    Leave blank fields if you don't want to update certain information.

    Note:
    Ratings for skills are must be between 1 and 5
  3. Click Submit Button
  4. Return to List:

    5. After submitting, you will be redirected back to the respective list.
