Adding Pets

To add a new pet, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to pet list.
  2. Click on the "Add New Pet" button.
  3. Fill out the required fields in the form provided.
  4. Submit the form.
  5. You will be redirected back to the list after successful submission.
